# Privacy and Compliance Guidelines

## Dataset Usage

This repository contains datasets for research and analysis purposes. The owner has confirmed that these datasets are allowed for research use.

### Datasets Included

The following types of datasets may be present in this repository:
- CSV files containing public records
- XLSX spreadsheets with structured data
- PDF documents with publicly available information

### Recommendations

1. **Data Protection**: Handle all datasets with appropriate care and in accordance with applicable data protection regulations.

2. **Research Use Only**: These datasets should be used exclusively for legitimate research, analysis, and educational purposes.

3. **Respect Privacy**: When working with personal information:
   - Minimize data exposure
   - Avoid unnecessary sharing or redistribution
   - Follow ethical research practices
   - Comply with local privacy laws and regulations

4. **Data Retention**: 
   - Do not modify or delete existing data files
   - Keep datasets intact for reproducibility
   - Document any derived data separately

5. **Ethical Considerations**:
   - Ensure research objectives are legitimate
   - Consider the impact of your analysis
   - Be transparent about data sources and methodology
   - Respect the rights of individuals in the datasets

## Compliance

Users of this repository are responsible for ensuring their use complies with:
- Local and international data protection laws (GDPR, PDPA, etc.)
- Institutional research ethics guidelines
- Terms of service of data sources
- Applicable privacy regulations

## Questions

For questions about dataset usage or compliance, please contact the repository owner.
